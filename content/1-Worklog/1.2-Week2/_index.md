### Week 2 Achievements:

* In-depth Understanding of Cloud Computing and the AWS Ecosystem: Mastered the core concepts of Cloud Computing, especially the flexible "Pay-as-you-go" pricing model. Recognized the competitive advantages of AWS (e.g., Economies of Scale) and explored its operational philosophy through Amazon's Leadership Principles.
* Proficiency in Architecture Design Tools (Cloud Visualization): Moved beyond theory by becoming familiar and proficient with draw.io combined with the standard AWS Architecture Icons. This enables sketching, planning, and presenting complex AWS system architectures visually, accurately, and professionally.
* Mastering Global Infrastructure and AWS Management Tools: Gained a thorough understanding of AWS's global physical architecture, including Regions, Availability Zones (AZs), and Edge Locations, thereby grasping the secrets to designing highly fault-tolerant and low-latency systems. Additionally, began using and distinguishing powerful management tools such as the AWS Management Console, AWS CLI, and SDK/API.
* Cost Management and Security (Shared Responsibility Model): Clearly understood the security boundaries between AWS and users through the Shared Responsibility Model. Grasped crucial tools for budget control and optimization like Cost Explorer and Trusted Advisor, and learned to classify AWS Support Plans suitable for real-world scales.
* Successfully Completing the Module 1 Hands-on Labs: Safely and successfully created an AWS Free Tier account and directly engaged in foundational lab exercises. This strongly honed self-study skills and the ability to consult official documents (AWS Documentation & Whitepapers) to confidently deploy and handle real-world scenarios.
